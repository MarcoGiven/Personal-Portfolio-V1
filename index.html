About Me

Hi! I'm nineteen-year old college student from North Plainfield, New Jersey. I've always loved anything technology related, from researching and building custom PCs and mechanical keyboards, to hacking and modding retro handhelds like my Nintendo 3Ds and PSVita, and a love and appreciation of video games. It became my passion in high school as a founding member/captain of our FIRST Robotics Team.

I'm currently a sophomore Computer Science student in Temple University's College of Science and Technology Honors Program. From my coursework, I'm skilled in Java, C, as well as math concepts up to Calculus II and Discrete Math. On my own, i've developed understandings in Python, HTML/CSS for web development, and some C# in Unity. I have experience in student leadership as an Honors Ambassadors to Temple Coordinator, and Honors Peer mentor. I'm Currently working in a project-based role as an Honors Program student worker, especially utilizing Tableau for data visualization for internal reports. Right now i'm actively engaged in university policy through the General Education Executive Committee, event organization, and technical projects.

Regardless, you'd still find me in my free time reading, learning guitar, or bouldering!
